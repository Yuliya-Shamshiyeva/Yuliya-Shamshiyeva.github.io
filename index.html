Top-5 Projects

My Screen Capybara

My favorite app. At the moment, it has no analogues in the stores. It's literally a capybara that lives on your smartphone screen and walks around from corner to corner. You can move it around the screen and change its clothes using skins inside the app. This little thing definitely brings joy to capybara lovers! And the stats say the same — more than 100k downloads on Google Play with a 4.8 rating!

Technologies: Kotlin, JSON, Firebase, CorelDraw, and many other things kept in strict secret :)

VOZDUKH Breathing Exercises

This application was my graduation project at Terricon School when I received additional training in Kotlin programming. The app helps not only to calm down and ground yourself but also supports and improves the condition of patients with COPD and other respiratory diseases. It was created specifically to help such patients. It is completely free and brings great results to users!

Technologies: Kotlin, JSON, Firebase, XML, Compose, Flow, After Effects, etc.

Almaty Seismic Fault Map

The app is based on official government data. It was created after strong earthquakes in the city. It allows residents to freely view fault lines, understand risks, decide whether it’s safe to buy an apartment in certain areas, and avoid misinformation.
